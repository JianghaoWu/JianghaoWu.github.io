Major hospitals are overcrowded while community clinics are often overlooked. Difficulties in seeking medical treatment, high medical expenses, and strained doctor-patient relationships have troubled a significant portion of the population. For instance, dealing with pleural effusion, many people opt to wait in long queues at large hospitals, causing various inconveniences.

In response to this, we have developed an Intelligent Chest Fluid Drainage Device and a Big Data Platform. This solution is characterized by its intelligence, user-friendliness, and simplicity, allowing people to access convenient, safe, and comfortable pleural effusion drainage services at community clinics.
