--- title: "Green Plant Air Purifier" excerpt: "A smart plant cultivation device that can intelligently nurture plants according to their growth, utilizing the plant's properties to achieve air purification.
" collection: portfolio --- As people's living standards improve and their desire for a better life grows, an increasing number of households are acquiring green potted plants. Additionally, due to the air-purifying properties of greenery, it's common for each household to have several pots of plants. However, the current state of home-based green plant cultivation has not yet achieved automation, and there are still many inconveniences. For urban dwellers with tight schedules, they may lack the time to care for plants, and they might also face situations such as business trips or vacations that keep them away from home for extended periods. Against this backdrop, we have designed an intelligent plant cultivation device that caters to the growth needs of plants, utilizing their natural properties for air purification. This device can be controlled through a mobile app, allowing users to manage lighting and purification functions.
